Overview

*Scorpion* is a thrilling drama series that follows a team of geniuses led by Walter O'Brien, who work together to solve complex global problems. Their extraordinary intelligence is often challenged by their personal issues, creating a blend of action, humor, and emotional depth.

Main Characters

  • Walter O'Brien: The brilliant leader and computer expert.
  • Tobias Curtis: The team's emotional support and strategist.
  • Happy Quinn: The mechanical prodigy and engineer.
  • Sylvester Dodd: The team's math whiz and worrywart.
  • Paige Dineen: The team's liaison to the real world.
  • Cabe Gallo: The government agent and team liaison who helps the team navigate various crises.

Episodes

The show consists of four seasons with various thrilling episodes, each presenting unique challenges that the team must overcome.
