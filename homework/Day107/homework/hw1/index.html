History of Basketball

Basketball was invented in 1891 by Dr. James Naismith in Springfield, Massachusetts. It has grown to become one of the world's most popular sports.

Rules of the Game

The game is played with two teams of five players each. The objective is to score points by shooting the ball through the opponent's hoop and you only have 24 seconds to do that.

Famous Players

Some of the greatest players in basketball history include Michael Jordan, LeBron James, Kobe Bryant, and Shaquille O'Neal.
