Guns

  • M16 Rifle - A lightweight, air-cooled, gas-operated, magazine-fed assault rifle.
  • AK-47 - A gas-operated assault rifle known for its durability and low production cost.
  • Glock 19 - A popular semi-automatic pistol used by military and law enforcement.

Ammunition

  • 5.56x45mm NATO - Used in M16 rifles and similar firearms.
  • 7.62x39mm - Standard round for AK-47 rifles.
  • 9x19mm Parabellum - Commonly used in handguns like the Glock 19.

Fighter Jets

  • F-22 Raptor - A fifth-generation, single-seat, twin-engine, all-weather stealth tactical fighter jet.
  • F-35 Lightning II - A family of stealth multirole fighters designed for various military roles.
  • Su-57 Felon - A fifth-generation Russian stealth fighter jet with advanced capabilities.

Bombers

  • B-2 Spirit - A stealth bomber designed for penetrating dense anti-aircraft defenses.
  • B-52 Stratofortress - A long-range, subsonic, jet-powered strategic bomber.

Tanks

  • M1 Abrams - A third-generation American main battle tank with advanced armor and weaponry.
  • T-14 Armata - A Russian main battle tank featuring an unmanned turret and advanced armor.

Naval Aircraft

  • F/A-18 Hornet - A twin-engine, all-weather tactical fighter and attack aircraft.
  • P-8 Poseidon - A military aircraft used for anti-submarine warfare and surveillance.

Submarines

  • USS Virginia - A class of nuclear-powered fast attack submarines.
  • Typhoon-Class - The largest class of submarine in the world, equipped with ballistic missiles.
